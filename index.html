Privacy Policy

App: Sudoku Rush · Developer: HV App Studio · Effective date: 05 September 2025 · Android

1) Overview

Sudoku Rush is a quick, casual 4×4 Sudoku game. We designed it to work without creating an account and to collect as little information as possible. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

2) What data is collected

a) Play Games Services (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games Services, Google provides us access to your Player ID, display name and profile icon to show your name on leaderboards and unlock achievements. We do not receive your email address or contacts. You can play without signing in.

b) Device & diagnostics

Basic, aggregated app diagnostics (crashes, ANRs, installs) may be available to us via Google Play Console. This information is anonymized and does not allow us to identify you.

c) Local game data

The app stores your scores, best times, level progress and simple settings locally on your device. Clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app will remove this local data.

3) What we do not collect

  • No account signup in the app.
  • No contact lists, photos, precise location, or microphone/camera data.
  • No advertising identifiers are used in this version.

4) Advertising & analytics

No ads in this version. We do not include AdMob or any advertising SDK right now. If we add ads or additional analytics in a future update, this policy will be updated and, where required, we will display the appropriate consent prompts.

5) How data is used

  • To display leaderboards and achievements through Google Play Games Services.
  • To keep your local game progress and preferences on your device.
  • To monitor app stability and quality via anonymized diagnostics from Google Play Console.

6) Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers necessary to run the app’s features (for example, Google Play Games Services). Their use of information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

7) Permissions

The app uses a basic network connection to access leaderboards and achievements. No special or sensitive device permissions are requested.

8) Children’s privacy

The game is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data to us, contact us and we will delete it.

9) Data retention

Local game data remains on your device until you clear the app’s data or uninstall. We do not keep personal data on our own servers. Any information handled by Google Play Games Services or the Google Play Console is retained under Google’s policies.

10) Your choices & rights

  • You may play without Play Games sign‑in.
  • You can sign out of Play Games at any time to stop sharing your profile with the game.
  • You can clear local data from the Android Settings ▸ Apps ▸ Sudoku Rush ▸ Storage ▸ Clear data.
  • For requests to access or delete data held by Google Play Games, please see Google’s privacy resources linked above.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted in the app listing or in‑app where appropriate. The “Effective date” at the top will reflect the latest version.
